Titan Watches, a foundational brand of the Titan Company Limited, is India's leading watchmaker and the fifth-largest integrated timepiece manufacturer in the world. Established in 1984 as a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO), Titan fundamentally transformed the Indian horology landscape by introducing high-quality, stylish quartz timepieces to a market previously dominated by mechanical watches. Over the decades, the brand has built a global footprint, exporting its precisi on-crafted designs to over 30 countries.
